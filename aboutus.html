How we did the project

⚙️Processing Scheme⚙

Here, we would like to share how we managed to create this website.

👨‍💻Data Collection👩‍💻‍

University Data are mostly collected from online sources. And for the consistency of the data and with the purpose of becoming a database-like collection for the successors, we store the collected data in the Excel sheets.

The only problem is that we do not have enough information to get access to all lists of universities in Myanmar.

🕵️‍♂️Data Research🕵️‍♀️️

Data Research is the main pipeline of this project and is also our team members' main focus. For the purpose of consistency, again, we manage to put in all the data we could research into a share sheet for re-usability. We would not want any data that we researched to be in vain after the project.

Sources from online were out-of-date and we have to find another ways round to get the updated and more accurate results.

🔖Task Allocation🔖

The team leader of the project is in charge of this area. Since each member has his or her own good points and weak points, tasks are carefully assigned to members within their capability.

Khant Thura is responsible for the project and its main theme and design. Wai Yan Moe Myint Lwin and Swam Pyae Sone Tun responsible for the innovative ideas and CSS design tweaks as well as JavaScript functions. The rest of members, being the main resources of information, responsible for collecting, gathering information and putting it into the HTML content.

👯‍♀️All-inclusive Teamwork👯‍♂️

We make sure that no one is left behind while building the project together. Believing that two is better than one we gather and exchange every member's point of view on every area of the project. With the tasks assigned to each member, we believe that every member may gain confident in answering the questions to their area of work and improve the knowledge of Web Development.

Since what we have learned from the teachers is not enough to create a real-world website, it is difficult to create an interface that we really expect. Self-study of individual member is the fundamental foundation of the project we are presenting.

⏺Project Goal⏹

Nothing is perfect, neither is our website. Since we are still at the basic steps of our web development journey, we understand that there are many flaws and unstable work under process throughout our website. We also want you to understand that.

However, we are youths with developing mindsets, and we never plan to stop growing this website. As far as goals are concerned, we have made the most of it clear for us to keep moving forward.

As we go on, we would like to:

  • implement a stable version and more reliable version of this website
  • widen our research area of universities throughout the whole Myanmar
  • ease off the confusion for high school graduates while deciding where to go after school
  • make a website that everyone can take a reference of
  • develop observation skills of each member in the team working on the Project
