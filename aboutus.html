ABOUT US

Get to know us!

Our Vision

This is a collection of information about the universities for matriculation students to get a correct choice of universities. We aim to help them with easier access to such information.

Our Mission

In the near furture, we will add
-detailed information of all the universities in Myanmar
-a way to let the users interact with one another

Our Story

Our developer team is formed with students from the first year of UIT. We have also dealt with ample challenges through deciding which university to choose after the matriculation exam. Therefore, our group got an idea to create a website that would help out these students by providing clear and easily accessible information of the universities. Hence, with our very best efforts, we have created this handy website.

How we did the project

Processing Scheme

Here, we would like to share how we managed to create this website.

Data Collection‍

University Data are mostly collected from online sources. For the consistency of the data and the further development, we store the collected data in the Excel sheets in a way of a database-like collection.

*We do not have enough information to give access to all the universities in Myanmar yet.*

Data Research

Data Research is the key to this project. For the purpose of consistency, again, we put all our research into a share sheet for re-usability. And so, no data that were researched is going to be in vain after this project.

*Due to out-of-date online sources, we had to find other ways round to get the updated and accurate results.*

Task Allocation

Tasks are carefully assigned to members within their capability by the team leader. Khant Thura, the leader, is responsible for the main theme and design of the project, Wai Yan Moe Myint Lwin and Martin, for their innovative ideas and CSS design tweaks as well as JavaScript functions and the rest of members, for the HTML contents and the major part, the information gathering.

‍All-inclusive Teamwork

We made sure that no one is left behind while building the projec. Believing that two is better than one, we gather and exchange every member's point of view on every area of the project. With the tasks assigned to each member, we believe that every member will gain confident in answering the questions to their area of work and improve the knowledge of Web Development.

Project Goal

Although, we are youths with basic developing mindsets, we will not be planning to stop evolving this website. As far as goals are concerned, we have made the most of it clear for us to keep moving forward.

As we go on, we would like to:

  • implement a stable version and more reliable version of this website
  • widen our research area of universities throughout the whole Myanmar
  • ease off the confusion for high school graduates while deciding where to go after school
  • make a website that everyone can take a reference of
  • develop observation skills of each member in the team working on the Project
