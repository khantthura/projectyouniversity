  • Accept 50% of boys and 50% of girls.
  • Applicant must be a high school graduate(science) student.
  • Applicant have good morality and be in proper health.

  • Applicant must be a Myanmar citizen holding(Naing)/(Pyu)/(Eh) NRC Card.

  • Applicant must fully attend the course at the allocated university by the department.

  • Applicant must agree that after the courses, he shall serve the mother country as public servant for at least (3) years.

  • If applicant do not follow the rules, he shall be fined up to 1 Million kyats as well as punished according to the existing rules and regulations.

  • Applicant must be a high school graduate student.

  • Applications to Yangon Technological University can be directly registered at Yangon Technological University, Kyo Kone, Insein.

  • The total of marks of Physics, Chemistry, Maths and English must exceed 320.

  • Applicant must be a Myanmar citizen holding(Naing)/(Pyu)/(Eh) NRC Card.

  • Applicant must be able to face challenging environments.
  • Applicant must be a high school graduate student.

  • Applicant must be a Myanmar citizen holding NRC Card. (must come up with copies.)

  • Applicant must follow the rules and regulations of the university.

  • Applications can be directly applied at the university.
